# 📊 Marketing Campaign Optimization & ROI Analysis

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using **Python, Excel, and Power BI** to identify high-performing channels, optimize marketing spend, and improve business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Companies invest heavily in marketing campaigns but often struggle to determine:

* Which campaigns generate the highest ROI
* Where budget is being wasted
* How to optimize marketing strategy

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* 🐍 Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) → Data Analysis
* 📊 Power BI → Dashboard & Visualization
* 📑 Excel → Data handling & preprocessing
* ⚙️ Power Query → Data Cleaning
* 📈 DAX → KPI calculations

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Marketing campaign dataset with:

  * Campaign
  * Channel
  * Spend
  * Revenue
  * Customers
* Includes **real-world issues**:

  * Missing values
  * Inconsistent channel names

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
* ROI (Return on Investment)
* Total Revenue
* Total Spend
* Profit

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed

* Data cleaning & transformation
* Channel-wise performance comparison
* Top and worst campaign identification
* ROI-based optimization analysis

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI cards (Revenue, Spend, ROI, CAC)
* ROI by Channel (Bar Chart)
* Spend vs Revenue comparison
* Top 5 campaigns table
* Interactive filters (Channel slicer)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* 📌 Email channel delivers highest ROI and lowest CAC
* 📌 Google generates highest revenue but requires higher investment
* 📌 Instagram campaigns show lowest performance
* 📌 Budget optimization can significantly improve profitability

---

## ✅ Recommendations

* Increase investment in Email & Google campaigns
* Reduce or optimize Instagram campaigns
* Focus on reducing CAC for better efficiency

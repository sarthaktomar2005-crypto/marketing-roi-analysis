1. Overall Performance:
The dataset shows strong marketing performance with positive ROI across most campaigns.

2. Channel Analysis:
- Email channel has the highest ROI, making it the most efficient channel.
- Google generates the highest revenue but comes with higher costs.
- Facebook shows moderate performance.
- Instagram has the lowest ROI, indicating underperformance.

3. Campaign Analysis:
- Top campaigns deliver significantly higher ROI and should be scaled.
- Low-performing campaigns should be optimized or discontinued.

4. Key Insights:
- High ROI channels should receive more budget.
- Inefficient campaigns reduce overall profitability.
- Strategic reallocation of budget can improve performance.

5. Recommendation:
- Increase investment in Email and Google campaigns.
- Reduce spending on Instagram campaigns.
- Focus on improving CAC.
